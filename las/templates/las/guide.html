LAS stands for Life as Someone Else. Life as Someone Else is a platform that allows for one group of users - presumably students - to be able to see the work/tasks that professionals do on daily basis.

Professionals can use Life as Someone Else to write diary entries of their day. This can be a chance to organize their thoughts or to give back to the community by providing a more personal perspective of their jobs rather than the generic information provided on the internet.

Non-professionals can learn about a profession on a personal level as they read about the daily activities of working professionals.

If you are a working professional and would like to share your work experience, press "Sign Up / Sign In".

If you would like to read posts from working professionals, press "Search By Field".

After logging in, professionals can start writing about their job, themselves, or daily work they do.

Non-professionals can explore jobs by each field on this category page. Search by a field and click on any profile to read the person's posts.
